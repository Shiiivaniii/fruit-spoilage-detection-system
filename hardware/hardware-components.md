# Hardware Components Used

## ESP32 Microcontroller
WiFi enabled microcontroller used as the main processing unit.

## DHT11 Sensor
Measures temperature and humidity.

## MQ2 Gas Sensor
Detects gases such as methane and hydrogen released during spoilage.

## MQ135 Gas Sensor
Detects ammonia, ethanol, and other volatile organic compounds.

## 16x2 LCD Display
Displays fruit freshness stage locally.

## Breadboard
Used for prototyping connections.

## Jumper Wires
Used for circuit connections.

## USB Power Supply
Provides power to ESP32 and sensors.
